About Us

Built on Craft. Driven by Passion.

Mr. Blessed Tint & Wrap was founded on a simple belief — every vehicle deserves a professional, precision install. Based in Princeton, TX, we serve the greater DFW area with top-quality window tinting and custom wraps.

We take the time to do things right. That means proper prep, quality film, and attention to every edge and corner. No bubbles, no peeling, no shortcuts.

Whether you are here for a basic tint or a full vehicle transformation, you will get the same level of care every time. That is the Mr. Blessed standard.

We are open 24 hours to fit around your schedule — not the other way around.
